Publications

Nov 2024

Neurodynamic Characterization and Prediction of Schizophrenia Using Echo State Networks with Serotonin Modulation: A Temporal and Frequency Band Analysis Approach

This study integrates serotonin modulation into Echo State Networks (ESNs) to analyze EEG data from schizophrenic patients. It improves predictive accuracy and reduces noise, particularly in frontal brain regions. Key findings include altered delta and gamma band activity, providing valuable insights into schizophrenia-specific EEG features that could enhance diagnostic methods.

Oct 2024

Enhancing Neurofuzzy Plasticity: A Fusion of LSTM and Artificial Neurogenesis

This paper presents a novel neuro-fuzzy network integrating Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) based Artificial Neurogenesis (ANG) to address the dead neuron and stability-plasticity dilemma. It achieves improved adaptability, scalability, interpretability, and accuracy over existing methods through extensive testing and performance evaluation. Published in IEEE Xplore as part of the conference proceedings of ICETCI 2024.
